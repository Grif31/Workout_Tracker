Privacy Policy

App: Aretē  ·  Developer: Griffin VonDeben  ·  Last updated: May 20, 2026

Short version: We collect only what's needed to run your workout tracker. We never sell your data, never show you ads, and never share your personal information with third parties except as described below. Your fitness data belongs to you.

1. What We Collect

Account information

  • Email address and username (required to create an account)
  • Display name and profile picture (optional, set by you)
  • Password — stored as a one-way hash; we never store or see your plaintext password

Fitness and health data

  • Workouts you log: name, date, duration, notes, exercises, sets, reps, and weight
  • Bodyweight entries you record
  • Personal records computed from your logged workouts
  • Exercise templates and routines you create

Location data (optional)

If you use the GPS cardio tracking feature, we collect your GPS route during the activity. Location access is only requested when you explicitly start a GPS tracking session. You can use all other features of the app without granting location permission.

Device preferences

Settings such as your weight unit (lbs / kg), rest timer duration, and color theme are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the app — displaying your workouts, computing stats and personal records
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • To generate AI workout suggestions (see Section 4)
  • To improve the app based on general usage patterns (no personal data is used for this)

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on servers hosted by a reputable cloud provider. We use industry-standard practices to protect it:

  • All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • Passwords are hashed using PBKDF2-SHA256 and are never stored in plaintext
  • Authentication uses short-lived JWT access tokens (15-minute expiry) with 30-day refresh tokens

No security system is perfect. If you discover a vulnerability, please contact us at the address in Section 8 and we will address it promptly.

4. Third-Party Services

Anthropic (AI workout generation)

When you use the AI Coach feature to generate a workout or routine, we send the following information to Anthropic's API: your selected training goal (e.g. Strength), experience level (e.g. Beginner), and days per week. No personally identifiable information (name, email, workout history) is shared with Anthropic. Anthropic's privacy policy is available at anthropic.com/privacy.

Apple Health & Google Health Connect (optional)

If you enable the Sync to Apple Health (iOS) or Sync to Health Connect (Android) setting, Aretē writes the following data to your device's on-device health store after each completed workout:

  • Exercise type (strength training or cardio)
  • Workout start time and end time

This data is written to Apple Health or Health Connect on your device only. It is never transmitted to our servers or shared with any third party. Aretē does not read any data from Apple Health or Health Connect — it only writes.

You can disable this integration at any time in the app's Settings screen. To remove workout sessions previously written by Aretē, use the Apple Health app (iOS) or the Health Connect app (Android).

Apple HealthKit commitment: Data obtained via Apple HealthKit is used solely to write workout sessions at your request. It is not used for advertising, data mining, or any purpose other than the health and fitness features described in this policy — in compliance with Apple's App Store Review Guidelines.

No advertising networks

We do not use any advertising SDKs or analytics services that track you across apps or websites.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not share your data with third parties except:

  • Service providers — infrastructure providers (hosting, database) who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality obligations
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users or the public

6. Your Rights and Data Deletion

You can, at any time:

  • Access your data — all your workouts and account info are visible in the app
  • Edit your data — update your profile, edit or delete individual workouts
  • Delete your account — contact us at the address in Section 8 and we will permanently delete your account and all associated data within 30 days
  • Export your data — contact us to request a copy of your data

7. Children's Privacy

Aretē is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
